  • Post 1: ISIYAYA


    Nakhu okurareje inengi lenu benazibuza bona khuyini Isiyaya begodu sivunulwa ngasiphi isizathu. Ngibone abanye kuma-comments bathi Isiyaya sivunulwa nguMalokazana oliKhosingwana (virgin bride), omunye wathi batjho iyafana ne-veil yesikhuwa. Mina ngithi AWAa akusilo iqiniso lelo.


    Ngizoyiphawula ngaphezulu ngombana ngangenelela izokuphothulula iNgoma yeQude neyoKwenda.


    Lalela, iSiyaya sivunulwa nguMalokazana, akukhethi bona uliKhosingwana nofana ubelethile. Ungangirhabi, ngilinda.


    IRhabi neYerhana neSiyaya is ONE AND THE SAME THING!

    IYerhena is the continuation of IRhabi,

    ISiyaya is the continuation of IYerhana.

    Umntwana omntazana nakaliTlawana uvunuliswa iRhabi,

    Wathomba uvunuliswa iYerhana,

    Wenda uvunuliswa iSiyaya.

    IRhabi ngeleTlawana likhamba noMdzegana munye noMrivathi. Why? Khumbula umthethwakho wangeQudeni.


    IYerhana ngeleThelerina likhamba neenrholwana zedinini neRaso. Why? Khumbula umthethwakho wangeQudeni.


    UNomhlekhabo waba nomntwana angakendi, abakamhlubulisi irhabi lakhe, bamhlubulise iYerhana kungakho ngithi iYerhena akusingelama Thari,


    Begodu ngikho ivunulo yaboLombeni inganaYerhana.


    Imbeleko. Fertility which is the 1 of 20 reasons/goals of Ukuthomba has been realized. AkusaYerhi, asisayerhisi, asisaRhabisi, we move unto the next level of womanhood which brings us to Isiyaya.


    Isiyaya ngesakaMalokazana sikhamba neenrholwana zedinini neDzila.


    ITlawana= Irhabi= Umdzegana=Umrivathi

    IThelerina= IYerhana= Iinrholwana zedinini= Iraso

    UMalokazana= ISiyaya= Iinrholwana zedinini= IDzila

    ko lapho eMkobola, Mkangala yiStayela soloko ibulala Isiyaya.

    Qala, nawuthombako awunikelwa iNtanga.


    Nawuyokwenda unikelwa iNtanga.


    Nawuphuma ekhenu uphuma umbethe isithimba sakho owasinikelwa nguGogwakho sitjhukwe nguBamkhulwakho lokha nawuyokubika bona uyokuthomba.


    Nawufika emzini uthola uNontende zakho, akuhlubulise isithimba sakho bese avunulise isithimba salapho esitjhukwe ngubamkhulu walapho.


    Kuthathwe iKumbese/Umbhalo wesokana lakho agega ngayo ephethe iNtanga yakhe bese uthweswa iNtanga leyo (yes, it's a ritual).


    Bese uthweswa ibhidi okuyiNgubo yabomma. Jama ngidlule la ngoba kuzakuvela iNgoma yokwenda iskiri.


    Bese uthweswe ISiyaya ngoba awusanalo iYerhana.


    Angeze wazi bona umsebenzi weSiyaya yini nangabe awuwazi umsebenzi weYerhana neRhabi.


    You see? After kubulewe iSiyaya, nasibuyako sesibuya nje kutlhogeka ilwazi kungakho kunabantu abaqabanga bona ISiyaya ngesamaVirgin. Just like last year, nenza iintayela nibeka amaPhogo phezkweentulo ende anifuni ukukhalinywa, 100 years later our great grandchildren will think kulisiko lesikhethu bona isokana lihlala phezkwesitulo they will even have PHOTOS that YOU took as evidence kanti abazi bona balahlekile.


    Okhunye kukuthi ISiyaya sihluka kathathu (3) azifani kodwana angeze wawubona umehluko as much as ungaboni bona iThelerina alivunuli ivunulo eyodwa iveke yoke, iya ngeengaba.


    Intwe le iyafana angeze wathola umlisa avunule uSonyana, nezipha nePoriyana ngesikhathi sinye. Each serve a specific purpose.


    Indaba yeSiyaya iyafana neyeenhluthu. Woke umuntu oya eNgomeni uyaguda noMalokazana nakaya eNgomeni yokwenda uyaguda kodwana kunabanye abafaka imigutjha batjho bona abagudi, ayikho intwenjalo ned kwaba nomunye ophaphako wabhala ukuguda wafaka umgutjha bese nabanye bamlandela then namhlanjesi uqabanga ukuthi sikhenu ukungagudi ihloko. Asikho IsiNdebele esinjalo. Nawuzokuzwisisa isiko-pilo leQude, nokuguda inhluthu uzokuzwisisa indaba yeSiyaya.


    Unfortunately it's too sensitive indaba yeSiyaya ned bengithi ngiyidlulise njengokuthembisa kwami bona ngizayidlulisa.


    Qala iRhabi, uqale iYerhana bese uQale iSiyaya. Look at the patterns bese ukhumbule iQude you shall find your answers.


    For further knowledge, await my book. I’m almost finished.

  • POST 2: Imiphambo enziwa babantu lokha nabenza umphahlo:


    1) Ukuphahla bambethe amanyethelo. Nawukhuluma nedlozi iinyawo zakho kufanele zithinte iphasi.


    2) Ukusela utjwala babezimu bangakaphahli. Utjwala buselwa bezimu qangi ngaphamb' kokuthi nina nibusele.


    3) Ukuphuma nge-Jarda nase baphahlile. Naniqeda ukuphahla anisaphumi ekhaya nanoma ningatlhoga into esitolo aniyi.


    4) Ukubanga itjhada ngokuvulela i-sound. Playing music batjho niyaditjha ngoba seniphahlile akukalungi ngoba naniqeda ukuphahla abezimu bafuna ukusebenza basebenzele phez'kwezinto ebenibabikela ngazo ye-ke abafuni ukusebenza ngetjhada.


    5) Ukulwa batjho nibamba umhlangano niya-solva. Nanithe niyokuphahla phahlani, ofuna umhlangano akabeke ilanga. Umphahlo awuhlanganiswa nezinye izinto.


    6) Ukuya emsamo unamavunda. Nawuya ebezimu woke umuntu kufanele aye ngehliziyo emhlophe. Lo wehliziyo engakahlali kuhle kufanele angayi ngoba nguye oyokusonga izinto.


    7) Ukulala kanye. Abezimu abalaliswa kanye, balaliswa kabili. Ngobusuku bokuthoma bayeza kodwana ingasi boke bese besibili beza boke baphelele. Nawulala kanye ungathola bona lo ekufanele akuphendule uza kusasa and wena sowukhambile then uthi abezimu abaphendulani kanti nguwe awenzi zona.

    Ukulala ngaphandle nofana ukuvalela. Woke umuntu kufanele alale ngendlini begodu kungabi khona umuntu ozozibandakanya kwezomseme. Angeze wabiza idlozi bona lizokubukela i-porn!


    9) Ukupheka sekurhwaqele. Latjhinga ilanga akusabi nembiza eziko. Imbiza ayitjhiswa amararho idlozi nase livukile. Akubi nento ephekwako, lidlozi kwaphela elizokuphehla.


    10) Isilwana silala ekhaya before ilanga lomphahlo. Nangabe niyokuphahla ngembuzi kufanele ifike namhlanjesi kube niphahla ksasa le yokuthi imbuzi ifika ngelanga lomphahlo ayenziwa.


    11) Last but not least, nawuphahlako uyazibika bona ungubani wakwabani negama lakho lokuthomba/lokuwela nokuthi ubelethwa ngubani nokuthi ubabizelani before ubalabala.


    Akwande.

  • POST 3: URhoboyi.


    Ngubani uRhoboyi?


    Language is power and the English language is the tool of Colonization. In our own African languages, we are free.


    Everything makes sense ngesintu and today I will break down the word uRhoboyi.


    URhoboyi yiNyanga ethiyelelwe ngesihlahla esithi esimila kokuthoma ngo-August bese sithi nasimilako iimpande kanye namakari wakhona ahlaza satjani (green) bese ithuthumbo lakhona limhlophe libe nekunubho etjheli (yellow). Isihlahlesi sitholakala khulu ePumalanga la kunzinze khona amaNdebele. Kungakho there’s a famous phrase from a poem ethi, “Ephasini laboRhoboyi neMbicana”


    URhoboyi ekukhulunywa ngaye nguye lo bese iMbicana mrorho othi nawusathelako uhlaza kwesibhakabhaka (blue-ish) bese nawutjhako uba-purple. IMbiqana sitjhebo kanti godu uyalapha, utlharulula iingazi, mhlambe nangabe iingazi zakho zijamile nofana zimahlwili (clots) umrorho lo uyatlharulula bese zigime ngefanelo eemthanjeni.


    NakunguRhoboyi (August) kutjhuguluka isikhathi somnyaka. Kuphela ubusika kungene iSilimela (Spring) lapho kuqubuka khona uRhoboyi. Ukutjhuguluka kwesikhathesi kuletha amatjhuguluko ebantwini ngombana soke siyiMvelo. Yeke isihlahla uRhoboyi has health benefits and I’ll lay down three:

    - Umzimba uyawa ngombana it has to adapt to a new season, therefore, children tend to get ill njengokuthi bathulule noMgomani. URhoboyi uyampheka bese useze abantwana, usiza ngokulekelela umzimba ukuze uqine ube sebujameni obufaneleko ngaphasi kwesikhathi esitjha.

    - Umntwana otshwenya ngokuzithundela uyabiliselwa naye uRhoboyi amrhamule bese uyalisa.

    - Uhlamba amazinyo. Lokha nawumhlafunyako njengeTjapisi ngombana wona vele usatjapisi ukhipha iinsalelo zokudla ezihlangana namazinyo, uqede nephunga ngemlonyeni. Ekadeni kwakuSilibazisamlomo.


    NgoRhoboyi kuba nento inengi abayibiza ngokuthi yi-Mercury Retrograde. Kuphela okhunye, kuthoma okhunye yeke ngalesi isikhathi izinto ezinengi ziyaphela kuthome ezinye kungakho kuba nokuhlangahlangana kwemizwa, kubuye nofana kukhambe abantu epilweni yomuntu nokuthi laba ebabhujelwe babelethi bagule bese kufanele basele amaRherhetjha.


    AmaRherhetjha ayaselwa begodu uyathunyisela ngawo khulu khulu lokha ngeNyanga le ekwabhubha umbelethi ngombana kulapho umbelethi waphenduka khona waba nguZimu bese nangoRhoboyi ngombana uRhoboyi uthanyela iinsila ngommoya wakhe ukulungiselela uKhukhulamungu la kuthuthumba khona amathuthumbo, kuhluma khona iintjalo begodu kuhlambuluka khona amanzi.


    AmaRherhetjha aselwa mumuntu obhujelwe babelethi nalo obhujelwe mntwana (miscarriage & stillborn). AmaRherhetjha ahlambulula both spiritually and physically iingazi nommoya womuntu ngombana there are spiritual ties between the living and the ancestors ekungakafaneli bona siwaqunte ngeenzathu ezinengi (let me not talk about spiritual ties).


    Yeke nakungena uRhoboyi kufanele wazi bona wenzani nawumumuntu. URhoboyi uthanyelile wabe waqubuka ukuze nakathanyelako ngommoya wakhe singalimali nje kufike uKhukhulamungu (September). Ngizakutjela bona uKhukhulamungu lo ngubani begodu wenzani yena.


    This post is late, but the one that breaks down September will arrive sooner. Siyathokoza Rhoboyi! August!
