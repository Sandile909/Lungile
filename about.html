Detailed Bio

Lungile Monica Mabena

A 24 year old enigmatic figure whose passion for art, culture, and spirituality is as multifaceted as her talents. As an Educator by profession (BEd), Writer, Journalist, International Poet, Youth Developer, Blogger, and Theatre Performer, she has consistently pushed boundaries and redefined the power of creative expression.

personal

Lungile is an Isintu restorer - a dedication to preserving and revitalizing the rich and diverse culture of Africa, Light carrier - a dedication to spreading the light of knowledge, wisdom, and creativity through the arts and Nurturer - a commitment to cultivating and supporting the growth of artists and the arts in Africa.

Her accomplishments span over a decade, from winning awards for her speech and poetry to organizing successful dialogues and hosting open mic sessions. Her blog "Ikesi Yakwanthu" and Facebook page with over 23, 500 followers showcase her commitment to promoting African culture, history, and wisdom.
