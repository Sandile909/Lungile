Hi, I'm Lungile Niica Kalon Mabhena.

I am multifaceted creative force who weaves words into inspiration. As a poet, I craft verses that capture the essence of the human experience, distilling emotions and thoughts into powerful, relatable language. With a passion for empowering others, I will take the stage as a motivational speaker, sharing messages of hope, resilience, and self-discovery that ignite sparks within audiences.
